So mend that fence and dog proof the garden now.

Here are a couple of other key things to consider.

Decide now on a small area where you can contain your puppy. (A dog can be kept in a room but puppies will sometimes need to be contained at night).

Think about buying a puppy pen, a crate or simply creating a safe place that you can put your puppy to rest when you need a break and where they sleep.

This safe place could be a laundry, bathroom or washroom.

Get hold of some toys, chews, and old objects that your puppy can chew safely for when they arrive home.

Decide now what areas you want your puppy or dog to be in and which areas are out of bounds.

This will then mean that you start off on the right step.
